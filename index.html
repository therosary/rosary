Our Mission

A prayer older than centuries

The Rosary is one of the most powerful prayers in Catholic tradition — a meditation on the life of Christ through the eyes of Our Lady. This page exists to guide, encourage, and equip every person who desires to pray more deeply, live more faithfully, and grow closer to God.

Whether you are praying for the first time or have faithfully prayed for decades, you are welcome here.

How to Pray the Rosary

A simple, step-by-step guide for beginners and a gentle reminder for those who pray daily.


Begin Today's Rosary
  • 1
    Apostles' CreedHold the crucifix and profess your faith.
  • 2
    Our FatherOn the first large bead.
  • 3
    Three Hail MarysFor faith, hope, and charity.
  • 4
    Announce the MysteryThen pray the Our Father to begin.
  • 5
    Ten Hail MarysMeditate on the mystery as you pray.
  • 6
    Glory BeEnd each decade with praise to the Trinity.
  • 7
    Repeat · Five DecadesClose with the Hail Holy Queen.
